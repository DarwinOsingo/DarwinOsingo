## Active Research

### `[PRES]` Kenya AI Executive Roundtable

> *Can fine-tuned LLM agents simulate evidence-based cabinet deliberation?*

Fine-tuned agents role-play Kenya's Cabinet Secretaries debating national policy, grounded in real Hansard records, CBK reports, IMF Article IVs, and Finance Acts. A six-dimension automated rubric — **KGS-R, KGS-F, RDS, ICS, PFS, ARS** — serves simultaneously as evaluation and GRPO reward function. Inter-agent citation conflicts are tracked live via a NetworkX debate graph. Human annotators: macroeconomics professors, philosophy PhDs, CS faculty.

**Target venues:** ICLR Africa Workshop · AAMAS 2026 · AfricaNLP 2026

**Stack:** `DeepSeek R1 Distill Qwen 14B` · `QLoRA / Unsloth` · `Qdrant` · `Voyage AI` · `BAAI Reranker` · `BM25 + RRF Fusion` · `FalkorDB` · `NetworkX` · `Groq`

---

### `[Sentinel]` Kenya Procurement Fraud Detection

> *Can graph AI surface what OAG audits flag but enforcement never reaches?*

Five-layer hybrid system over Kenya's PPIP/OCDS tender data: temporal knowledge graph (FalkorDB), 23 Cypher red-flag rules, Leiden community detection, positive-unlabeled XGBoost, and a ReAct LLM reasoning agent. Ground truth validated against EACC annual reports, OAG audit findings, and judiciary records. Framed against the EACC figure of **KSh 608 billion** (~7.8% of GDP) in annual corruption costs.

**Evaluation target:** Recall@500 → 0.75–0.88

**Stack:** `FalkorDB / Neo4j` · `XGBoost (PU Learning)` · `Leiden Community Detection` · `Jamba` · `RAG Pipeline` · `tenders.go.ke OCDS`

---

### `[K-Claw]` Kenya AI Skill Pack

> *Local-first AI tooling for Kenyan financial compliance, built on the OpenClaw framework.*

Skills covering M-Pesa Daraja, KRA iTax, KRA eTIMS, NHIF, and NSSF — running Gemma 4 locally via Ollama, fine-tuning target phi4-mini:3.8b via QLoRA for low-spec hardware. Communicates over WhatsApp. Kenya's first OpenClaw-native skill pack.
