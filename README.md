## Research

### Kenya AI Executive Roundtable (PRES)

> Multi-agent simulation of Kenya's President and Cabinet Secretaries debating national policy

Fine-tuned LLM agents role-play Kenya's Cabinet using real Hansard Q&A data. Built on a hybrid RAG retriever over 100K+ document chunks, live inter-agent citation conflict tracking via NetworkX, and a six-dimension automated evaluation rubric — KGS-R, KGS-F, RDS, ICS, PFS, ARS — that doubles as a GRPO reward function and is validated against real parliamentary records.

**Stack:** `DeepSeek R1 Distill Qwen 14B` · `QLoRA via Unsloth` · `Qdrant` · `Voyage AI Embeddings` · `BAAI Reranker` · `BM25 + RRF Fusion` · `Groq` · `NetworkX`

---

### Kenya Procurement Corruption Detection

> Graph-plus-RAG fraud detection over Kenya's public procurement pipeline

Maps supplier and contractor networks from structured OCDS data to surface shell companies, linked director networks, and inflated tenders. Targets the structural enforcement gap between OAG audit findings and actual recovery. Ground truth validated against EACC annual reports, OAG audit findings, and judiciary conviction records — framed against the EACC National Ethics Survey figure of KSh 608 billion (~7.8% of GDP) in annual corruption costs.

**Stack:** `Jamba (Mamba-Transformer hybrid)` · `FalkorDB / Neo4j` · `RAG Pipeline` · `tenders.go.ke OCDS Data`

---
